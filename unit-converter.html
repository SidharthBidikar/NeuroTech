Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the difference between the Metric and Imperial systems?

The **Metric System** is a decimal-based system (using powers of 10) with base units like the meter, gram, and liter. It's the standard for most of the world. The **Imperial System** (or U.S. Customary System) uses units like feet, pounds, and gallons, which have more complex relationships (e.g., 12 inches in a foot, 16 ounces in a pound).
